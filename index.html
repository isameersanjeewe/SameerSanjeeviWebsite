About

Hey there! This is Sameer and thank you for visiting my website. Over the last 3+ years being an entrepreneur, engineer and an artist, my biggest takeaway has been understanding Customers, Creativity, Effective Communication, Design Thinking, Curiosity, Conflict Resolution and most importantly creating a Win-Win situation for all stakeholders in a Business.
While building an IoT based tech startup, I had the opportunity to switch multiple hats from being a design engineer to a project manager. In this process I enjoyed being at the interlace of technology and business, evetually I decided to pursue my Master’s in Management Information Systems to understand more about Data, Design and Business thereby, helping me pursue new opportunites and challenges.
Please feel free to check out my resume to know more about my qualifications and work experience.

I love being around people as much as being around technology, one of my biggest strengths has been the ability to understand people and build meaningful relationships. This allowed me to solve problems in real-life as well in business, be it travelling to a new place, working with cross-functional teams or ability to empathize with user needs and customer pain points.
I have always strongly believed to not take anything for granted, I’m personally driven towards minimizing wastage be it energy or food. I’m quite passionate towards sustainable living and how can science help in making the world better and green. Be it projects during my undergraduate in engineering or my first job it has always been to move towards sustainability optimizing existing process and ways to save resources.
Other things that are close to my heart are Music, Beatboxing, and Cooking (I cook really good authentic South-Indian food).

Beatboxer

A self learnt skill that started as a hobby soon became a side profession. Beatboxing allowed me to use the human voice to explore and create different sounds by channeling my creativity to music. It has also given me an opportunity to be a teacher, digital content creator and even model for a merchandise company 😛

As a performing artsit for the last 9 years, performing at various stages for 100 to 3000+ audiance helped me develop various real-life skills from public speaking to marketing. Being a solo artist perfoming I was able to work with small event companies performing for various curated shows, corparte shows and also collaborate with other musicians.
