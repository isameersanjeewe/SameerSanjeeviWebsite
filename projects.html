Projects

Thermlegy

Thermelgy- is an IoT based platform aimed to monitor energy and equipments for HVAC applications. The platform gets data from edge devices and provides the user with customised BI thereby helping them improve the operational efficency through direct control of the equipments all remotely. The direct and indrect savings achieved by the user through monitoring and managing their process is what drives thermelgy to enable energy as aservices.
As a founding member and a Product Manager, I wore multiple hats from gathering requirements from various stakeholder to setting up Product Road Maps and developing the Web Application.

Cloud-Pulze

CloudPulze- is an cloud cost optimsation tool, the current verison is compatble with GCP and it measures the performance and helps start and stop cloud servies like cloud instances and kubernetes engines to optimse cost .
As a Project Manager I had cooridanted the entire business logic by understanding diffrent API's on GCP gathering requirments and helping develop the front end of the application.
